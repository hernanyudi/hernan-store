Jackson Pro Series Signature Misha Mansoor Juggernaut HT6 Electric Guitar

First introduced in 1976, the StingRay has been revered as one of the most iconic bass guitars in history. The StingRay was the first production four-string bass to feature onboard active equalization. The flagship of the Music Man line, today's StingRay retains the same signature features that it had some forty years ago, including a solid roadworthy construction, iconic oval pickguard, 3+1 tuning key configuration, and the ever-popular Music Man humbucker — all of which combine to produce a look, feel, and remarkably unmistakable sound.

Ibanez PGM50 Paul Gilbert Signature Electric Guitar, Black

The Ibanez PGM50, introduced in 2023, is a solid-body electric guitar belonging to the PGM series and serves as a signature model for American guitarist Paul Gilbert. Crafted in Indonesia, it takes its place in the mid-tier Premium line, showcasing a blend of quality craftsmanship and Paul Gilbert's distinctive design preferences. The PGM50 highlights an American basswood body with painted f-holes, bolted to a maple and walnut neck. It boasts a 22-fret rosewood fingerboard with white dot position markers and side dots. Featuring DiMarzio pickups, the configuration includes a pair of DiMarzio Air Classic humbuckers alongside a DiMarzio PGM single-coil. Other components comprise an F106 fixed bridge, gold hardware, and Gotoh MG-T locking machine heads.
